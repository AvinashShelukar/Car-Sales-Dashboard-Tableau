

# 🚗 Car Sales Dashboard | Tableau Project

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Car Sales Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence solution developed using Tableau to analyze and monitor automobile sales performance. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key sales metrics, customer purchasing patterns, and brand performance, enabling stakeholders to make informed, data-driven decisions.

This project demonstrates the application of data visualization and business analytics techniques to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights. Through interactive charts, filters, and KPI indicators, users can explore sales trends and evaluate business performance across different dimensions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this dashboard are:

* Monitor overall car sales performance through key performance indicators (KPIs).
* Analyze monthly and yearly sales trends.
* Identify top-performing car brands and models.
* Evaluate revenue generation and sales distribution.
* Enable interactive exploration of sales data through dynamic filters.
* Support strategic business decision-making with visual insights.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. KPI Monitoring

The dashboard provides real-time tracking of essential business metrics, including:

* Total Cars Sold
* Total Revenue Generated
* Average Selling Price
* Year-over-Year Performance Comparison
* Growth Rate Analysis

### 2. Sales Trend Analysis

Visual representations help identify:

* Monthly sales performance
* Seasonal sales patterns
* Growth and decline trends
* Peak sales periods

### 3. Brand Performance Analysis

Users can analyze:

* Best-selling car brands
* Revenue contribution by brand
* Market share comparison
* Brand-wise sales distribution

### 4. Model Performance Analysis

The dashboard highlights:

* Top-performing vehicle models
* Sales volume by model
* Revenue generated per model
* Customer preferences and purchasing trends

### 5. Interactive Filtering

Users can dynamically filter data based on:

* Car Brand
* Model
* Year
* Region (if available)
* Sales Category
